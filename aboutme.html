My Story:

Hello and welcome to my page! This a place where I can showcase my love for vintage, whether it's music, clothes, figures, posters, home decor, or anything alike. It may sound very basic but I've been involved with the thrifted scence my whole life. My mother was a Goodwil manager for several years, while my dad worked at a fruit a farm and picked oranges and avocatos. I've pretty much been decked out in thrifted gear since I've been born and even when we moved from SoCal to the Austin area in 2005, my mom and I still would hit the thrifts. Lukily now I've been able to turn this into a small business where I can now buy the pieces that I want as well as make a little bit of bread on the side. I appreciate anyone who's followed my page, liked my pictures, messaged me through Instagram, stopped to look at my clothes at the pop ups I've been a part of, took the time to talk to me and get to know me, and to those that have bought from me. The support is always appreciated and I always trive to give the best service possible and be as genuine as I can be. Thank you.
